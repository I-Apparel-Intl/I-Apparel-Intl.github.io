# 🔐 How to Login

**Follow the steps below to log in to LineGuru on your device.**

---

<div align="center">
  <img src="/img/login.jpeg" width="50%" alt="Login Screen" />
</div>

---

## 📋 Login Steps

### 1️⃣ Step 1 — Enter the URL

Type the server address in the **URL** field.

> Example: `http://localdomain` or `http://ipaddress`

<div align="center">
  <img src="/img/url.png" width="50%" alt="Enter URL" />
</div>

---

### 2️⃣ Step 2 — Enter Your User ID

Type your User ID in the **Enter Your ID** field.

:::tip QR Code Shortcut
Tap the **QR code icon** on the right to scan your ID automatically — faster and error-free.
:::

<div align="center">
  <img src="/img/id.png" width="50%" alt="Enter User ID" />
</div>

---

### 3️⃣ Step 3 — Enter Your Password

Type your password in the **Enter Your Password** field.

> Tap the 👁️ eye icon to show or hide your password.

<div align="center">
  <img src="/img/pd.png" width="50%" alt="Enter Password" />
</div>

---

### 4️⃣ Step 4 — Remember Me *(Optional)*

Check the **Remember Me** checkbox to save your login details for next time.

> ✅ Red checkmark = enabled

<div align="center">
  <img src="/img/remedme.png" width="50%" alt="Remember Me" />
</div>

---

### 5️⃣ Step 5 — Submit

Tap the red **SUBMIT** button to log in.

<div align="center">
  <img src="/img/sumit.png" width="50%" alt="Submit" />
</div>

---

## 💡 Quick Tips

| | Tip | Details |
|---|---|---|

| 📷 | Use QR Scanner | Faster and error-free ID entry |
| 🔒 | Remember Me | Save your login for next time |
