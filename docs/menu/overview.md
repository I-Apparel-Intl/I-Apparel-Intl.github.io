# 📖 Overview

## What is LineGuru?

LineGuru is a cutting-edge iOS app designed to transform the garment manufacturing industry by streamlining the production process from cutting to final assembly.

Our app integrates seamlessly with your production line, enabling efficient management, tracking, and optimization of garment production.

With LineGuru, manufacturers can ensure high-quality output, reduce errors, and enhance overall productivity.
